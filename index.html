KhetGuru

KhetGuru will be a pioneering agricultural project to transform farming practices through the integration of technology and community support. This innovative initiative combines soil sensors, data analytics, and a community-driven mobile application to optimize crop selection and promote knowledge sharing among farmers.

Hardware

The hardware will incorporate a range of sensors, including NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium), Moisture, Water Level, and pH with ARDUINO as microcontroller, to provide an in-depth analysis of soil composition. These sensors will be combined to make a single device that can be strategically placed in farmlands to monitor critical soil parameters.

Software

The software will have an advanced data analytics engine which processes the data from the sensors and generates personalized crop recommendations for each farmer based on their specific soil conditions. This data-driven approach assists farmers in making informed decisions about the crops they should cultivate.

Set the device on your soil.

Set our device on your soil so that our sensors reach inside. The device will then analyze the soil and provide various details about the soil such as composition of macronutrients and pH of the soil.

Enter the details in our app.

Enter your preferences and the details of your soil analyzed by the device in our app which has a multitude of supported languages and a user-friendly design.

Get all your crop recommendations.

The app will then use various algorithms to further analyze the data to provide you with a report recommending the best crops to grow on the soil along with estimated costs and requirements.
